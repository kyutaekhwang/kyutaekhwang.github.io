User Experience Designer

I have a keen interest in understanding the intricacies involved in human behavior and decision making. As a User Experience Designer, I focus on this interest to analyze users’ decision making process and create enjoyable products based on research.

Research tools include user surveys, interviews, contextual analysis, and usability testing. Design tools include design studio, wireframes and prototypes. I mostly use Sketch and Invision, often dabbling in Front-End.

Feel free to reach out! I'm always looking to meet new people and experience new things. You can send a message to my  inbox  or call me at my  number .
